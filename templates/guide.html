User Guide

Getting Started

GardenAI Advisor helps you identify plant diseases and provides personalized care recommendations for your home garden.

  1. Select your preferred language from the dropdown
  2. Choose your plant type or use auto-detect
  3. Upload a clear photo of your plant's leaves
  4. Click "Analyze Plant" to get instant results

Best Practices for Photos

  • Lighting: Take photos in natural daylight for best results
  • Focus: Ensure leaves are in clear focus
  • Distance: Capture close-up shots of affected areas
  • Background: Use a plain background when possible
  • Multiple angles: If unclear, analyze from different angles

Understanding Results

Confidence Score: Shows how certain the AI is about the detection (0-100%)

  • 90-100%: Very confident - follow recommendations
  • 70-90%: Confident - good accuracy
  • Below 70%: Consider taking another photo or consulting expert

Healthy Plants: Receive care tips for maintaining health

Diseased Plants: Get treatment options and prevention strategies

Important Disclaimers

  • All recommendations are advisory only
  • Consult local gardening experts for critical decisions
  • Test treatments on small areas first
  • Follow product instructions carefully
  • Results may vary based on local conditions

Tips for Home Gardening

  • Regular Monitoring: Check plants weekly for early disease detection
  • Proper Spacing: Ensure adequate air circulation between plants
  • Clean Tools: Disinfect gardening tools to prevent disease spread
  • Quality Soil: Use good potting mix for container gardens
  • Water Wisely: Water at soil level, avoid wetting leaves
  • Organic First: Try organic solutions before chemical treatments
