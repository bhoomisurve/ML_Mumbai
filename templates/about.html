About GardenAI Advisor

Our Mission

Empowering home gardeners and urban farmers with AI-powered plant health insights and personalized gardening recommendations.

GardenAI Advisor makes advanced agricultural technology accessible to everyone, whether you're growing vegetables on your balcony, maintaining a small kitchen garden, or cultivating plants in limited urban spaces.

Technology Stack

AI & Machine Learning
  • Vision AI: DenseNet-based deep learning models
  • Decision AI: Google Gemini for recommendations
  • 9 Specialized Models: One for each crop type
Backend & Storage
  • Framework: Flask (Python)
  • Database: MongoDB Atlas
  • Image Processing: TensorFlow & PIL

Key Features

Image Analysis

Advanced computer vision detects diseases with high accuracy from plant photos

Location-Based

Automatic location detection for region-specific recommendations

Weather Integration

Real-time weather data influences watering and care advice

Multi-Language

Support for 10+ Indian languages for wider accessibility

How It Works

  1. Image Upload: You upload or capture a photo of your plant
  2. Vision Agent: AI analyzes the image using trained deep learning models
  3. Location Agent: System detects your location and fetches weather data
  4. Decision Agent: Gemini AI synthesizes all data to generate recommendations
  5. Results: You receive actionable, context-aware gardening advice

Who Can Benefit

  • Urban Gardeners: Growing plants in apartments and balconies
  • Home Gardeners: Maintaining kitchen gardens and small plots
  • Hobby Farmers: Learning sustainable gardening practices
  • Students: Studying plant health and agriculture
  • Community Gardens: Managing shared growing spaces

Responsible AI Usage

Important: GardenAI Advisor provides advisory recommendations only. Always:

  • Test treatments on small areas first
  • Follow product safety instructions
  • Consult local experts for critical decisions
  • Consider organic solutions before chemicals
  • Monitor plant response to treatments

Model Performance

Our DenseNet121-based models are trained on thousands of plant images with the following characteristics:

  • Input size: 256x256 RGB images
  • Transfer learning architecture for efficiency
  • Multi-class classification per crop type
  • Optimized for mobile and web deployment
